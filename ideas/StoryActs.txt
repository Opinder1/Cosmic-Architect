Game is split into 5 acts

The acts level boundaries are as follows:
Act 1: level 1-100 (100 levels) (Student or mercenary in high civilization)
Act 2: level 101-250 (150 levels) (Soldier or top mercenary in high level civilization)
Act 3: level 251-450 (200 levels) (Top power in high level civilization)
Act 4: level 451-700 (250 levels) (Demigod)
Act 5: level 701-1000 (300 levels) (Divine level)
